How to Use ISUStudySpots Dropdown Menu*


To use the dropdown menu(located at the top of the home screen), tap or click on the "Buildings" option.



A list of buildings should appear. Scroll through the options to find the building you are looking for and tap or click on it.


Next, tap or click on the "Location Name" option.



A list of study locations for the building you selected should appear. Tap or click on the location you wish to view, and you will be redirected to a page with more information about that location.


*If you are not seeing building names or locations, the dropdown menu may not be compatible with your device. This may be the case if you have an Iphone 5 or older Iphone.
